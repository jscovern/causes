March 2015
Pandas Fight for Love in the Face of Panda Express's Armed Guards

Panda Express's militarized private police force took to the streets on the morning of Monday, March 3rd 2015.

Pandas, adorned with a red heart on their chests, made their way past the brute police forces. They occassionally reached out for hugs, but only recieved cold stares from the armed forces. All in all, Panda Express was not impressed and made no moved to release the pandas from their contractual logo agreements.
